Account Deletion Request

If you wish to delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

Steps to Request Account Deletion:

  1. Send an email to theparkitofficial@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, include your account details (e.g., name, email address, and any other identifying information).
  3. We will process your request and notify you once your account and data have been deleted.

Data Deletion Information:

Upon account deletion, we will delete the following data:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email, phone number).
  • Booking history and preferences.
  • Location data associated with your account.

Some data may be retained for legal or accounting purposes, such as transaction records, if required by law.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at theparkitofficial@gmail.com.
